About Us

We are a client-focused, result oriented, and relationship driven law firm. Insight and strategic thinking are core to our practice. We take the time to fully understand not only our client’s business but the industry in which they operate as well. We strive to keep abreast of new and emerging litigation, laws and regulations.

Our practice encompasses a full range of corporate and commercial legal services. In addition, we engage in robust and creative litigation and we equally offer a distinct alternative dispute resolution practice. The Firm provides advice and representation to clients who desire to resolve disputes in mediation, arbitration, or other alternative dispute resolution processes. Also, we advise clients at the time of contract formation, about the merits of various dispute resolution processes and how to achieve the greatest benefits through contract language.

Our litigation practice is committed to bringing value by offering creative, timely and strategic advice to help solve complex legal issues and achieve our clients’ business goals. We routinely collaborate with clients to develop litigation strategies using early case assessment methods and then implement the plan with efficiency. We strive to produce not only desired results for our clients at trial or on appeal but also encourage and skillfully negotiate settlements when their interests are best served by resolution short of the courthouse.

We advise clients (governmental agencies, parastatals, private and public corporations) on policy formulations and help to develop Codes of Ethics and other related internal policies and procedures. Furthermore, we help our clients to develop training materials, programs, and conduct ethics and compliance training tailored to meet clients’ particular needs. We know Organizational Conflicts of Interest and help our clients to develop strategies for avoiding or mitigating them. Our clients get quality advice on how to remain in compliance without sacrificing commercial competitiveness. We are located in Abuja- the Federal Capital Territory with operations across major cities in Nigeria. We aspire to be the leading transactional law firm with a practice spanning across Africa and beyond.
